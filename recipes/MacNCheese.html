Prepared Mac and Cheese

Macaroni and cheese is a dish of cooked macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar sauce.

Traditional macaroni and cheese is a casserole baked in the oven; However, it can be prepared in a sauce pan on top of the stove or using a packaged mix.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Add water to pot in use and let it boil. Place Macaroni in pot and let it cook for around 10 mins.
  2. In a separate pan, mix the butter, flour, milk, cheese, salt, pepper and paprika together. Let it simmer and boil
  3. Add cooked macaroni to pan with simmering mixture. Mix thoroughly and allow to sit for around 5 mins.
  4. Serve Hot and enjoy!
