Shakshouka

Prepared Shakshouka Dish

Shakshuka (chakchouka or shakshouka) is a middle eastern dish of tomatoes, onions, pepper, spices, and eggs. It's usually eaten for breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell Peppers
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Chili pepper
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Heat Olive oil in pan to use, and stir in eggs to make them scrambled
  2. Add in onion, bell pepper, garlic and stir until they become soft
  3. Mix in a seperate bowl tomatoes, chili peppers, cumin, paprika, and salt.
  4. Stir in mixture in seperate bowl into the pan with the eggs.
  5. Contiune to stir until mixture is throughouly mixed and desired consistency for scrambled eggs is reached.
  6. Serve Hot and enjoy!
